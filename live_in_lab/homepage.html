Our website helps farmers and gardeners choose the right crops and learn about their soil. Users can enter details like the type of crop, soil, and fertilizer they want to use. Then, they get personalized suggestions on the best crops for their specific conditions. We also have a cool feature where users can upload photos of their soil or plants. Our system then analyzes these images to show how the plants are growing or what type of soil is in the picture. This helps users make better decisions and improve their farming results
